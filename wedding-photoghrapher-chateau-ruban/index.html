WEDDING PHOTOGRAPHER CHÁTEAU RÚBAŇ

WEDDING AT CHÁTEAU RÚBAŇ

We cannot be more thankful for wonderful people that we met during our photography journey.
When Johanka and Klemens invited us as wedding photographers to capture their wedding day at Château Rúbaň – gorgeous vinery located 1 hour drive from Bratislava, we knew it’s going to be a special one.

Their wedding was filled with warmth (pun intended, it was one of the hottest day in August), love and care.
It was so captivating to watch how each family member and guest was so emotionally invested in being there for the couple.

ROMANTIC CELEBRATION AT CHÁTEAU RÚBAŇ

It won’t be an exaggeration to say that we felt that day like a part of this wonderful slovak-german family.
Thanks to this incredible trust they gave us we could focus on our job. And what’s the most important, Johanka and Klemens could truly be present for their guests, not worrying about anything.

SPECIAL THANKS

Last but not least we wanted to thank Andrej REDVISION Wedding who was responsible for capturing the video from that day which you can check here. It was a blast working with you and we hope to meet you again some day!
