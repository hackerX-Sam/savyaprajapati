Master the Dark Aesthetic: Wedding Workshops for Photographers in Europe

Nostalgia Workshop The 5th Edition: Wedding Workshops for Photographers in Europe Returns to Poland

We are thrilled to announce that the 5th edition of Nostalgia, our exclusive wedding workshops for photographers in Europe is returning to one of our favorite locations: Poland.

This upcoming edition will take place within the historic walls of the Castle of the Knights of St. John in Łagów(Zamek Joannitów). This isn’t just a venue; it is our home for the duration of the event. We have secured exclusive access to the entire castle for four full days, ensuring total privacy and creative freedom.

Theory, Practice, and Masters

We believe that true growth comes from a balance of knowledge and action. That is why this workshop is carefully structured to include both intensive theory and hands-on practice. You will be shooting alongside us, learning how to master light and composition in real-time.

This edition features incredible guest mentors:

  • Studio Babel: Passionate about the timeless art of analog photography, they will teach you how to integrate the raw, organic look of film into your work.
  • Braulio Lara: A master of storytelling and cinematic imagery, Braulio will guide you on how to capture emotion that feels like a scene from a movie.

Curated Aesthetics: Vintage Details and Florals

Every corner of the castle will be transformed into a portfolio-building paradise. We have prepared breathtaking stylings, including:

  • Lush Florals: Deep, moody arrangements inspired by Dutch Masters paintings.
  • Vintage Details: Carefully selected antique decor and custom vintage stationery.
  • Decadent Sweets: Styled dessert tables that look too good to eat.

We take care of the “what” so you can focus on the “how.” You will leave with a cohesive, high-end gallery that attracts your dream clients.

An Immersive Experience: Polish Hospitality

This experience goes beyond just photography. It is a retreat for your soul. Expect true Polish hospitality with an abundance of delicious, homemade food—we believe that great conversations happen over great meals.

After a long day of shooting, we will gather for intimate evenings by the castle fireplace. It is the perfect time for networking, sharing stories, and relaxing with a glass of wine in hand. If you are looking for wedding workshops for photographers in Europe that offer community, art, and a bit of magic, join us in Łagów.
