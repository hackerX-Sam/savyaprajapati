ICELAND ELOPEMENT PHOTOGRAPHY

ELOPING IN ICELAND

Iceland’s wild, otherworldly beauty has long made it a dream destination for couples looking to elope, and Monika and Szymon’s journey from Poland proved just how magical it can be. With its dramatic landscapes and secluded spots, Iceland offers the perfect backdrop for intimate vows and breathtaking photography.

A LOVE STORY IN THE LAND OF FIRE AND ICE

Monika and Szymon, a couple who share their lives fostering rescue dogs in Poland, decided to turn their dream of an Icelandic elopement into reality. Drawn by the country’s iconic landscapes, they set out to create an unforgettable experience—one that was uniquely theirs.

A SCENIC ROADTRIP ACROSS ICELAND

Their elopement began at the stunning Panorama Glass Lodge, where they got ready while soaking in views of Iceland’s vast wilderness. From there, we embarked on a road trip across the south and southwest, visiting some of Iceland’s most iconic spots:
• Seljalandsfoss: A majestic waterfall that provided a misty, magical setting.
• Reynisfjara Beach: Where heavy rain added drama to the black sands.
• Dyrhólaey: A striking cliffside with sweeping ocean views.
• Stokksnes: The secluded and serene site they chose for their vows.

Stokksnes, often overlooked compared to the famous Reynisfjara, felt like a private haven—completely untouched by crowds and perfect for an intimate vows exchange.

AUGUST: A PERFECT MONTH
FOR ICELAND ELOPEMENTS

Eloping in Iceland at the end of August was a dream. Despite a rainy spell at Reynisfjara, the warm temperatures allowed us to work comfortably while embracing the ever-changing weather. August and early September remain our favorite times for elopements in Iceland, offering a perfect mix of lush landscapes and manageable conditions.

WHY WE LOVE PHOTOGRAPHING ICELAND ELOPEMENTS

Working with Monika and Szymon was effortless—they were deeply in love and fully trusted us, which allowed us to capture their genuine connection without interruption. Iceland’s unpredictable weather can be challenging, but it’s also part of its charm. We always plan extra time for our couples to ensure we can adapt if conditions call for flexibility.

PLANNING YOUR ICELAND ELOPEMENT

If you’re considering Iceland for your elopement, here are our top tips:

• Allow Extra Time: Plan to spend more time at each location to truly soak in the experience without feeling rushed.
• Stay Flexible: Iceland’s weather is unpredictable, so being adaptable is key to a stress-free elopement.
• Choose Unique Spots: Iceland is filled with hidden gems like Stokksnes, where you can enjoy complete seclusion for your vows.

We also offer personalized recommendations for vendors and help you navigate the logistics of planning an elopement in Iceland.

YOUR ICELAND ELOPEMENT
DREAM STARTS HERE

Iceland offers endless possibilities for creating a day that’s entirely your own. From its dramatic waterfalls to quiet, untouched landscapes, the Land of Fire and Ice is the perfect stage for an unforgettable elopement.
